Frequently Asked Questions

How does Unlicode ensure privacy?

Unlicode uses local LLMs, meaning your code and prompts never leave your machine. All AI interactions happen locally, ensuring complete privacy.

What does unlimited agent usage mean?

Unlike other IDEs that limit AI interactions, Unlicode allows you to use AI agents as much as you want without hitting token limits or usage caps.

Is Unlicode really free and open source?

Yes! Unlicode is completely free and open source. You can view, modify, and contribute to the codebase on GitHub.
