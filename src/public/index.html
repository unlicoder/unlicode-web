Frequently Asked Questions

How does unlimited tokens work?

Unlicode runs AI models locally on your machine, so there are no external API calls or token limits. You can run as many prompts as your hardware can handle.

What hardware do I need?

Unlicode works on CPU, GPU, and HPU setups. For optimal performance, we recommend at least 16GB RAM and a modern multi-core processor.

Is my code really private?

Yes! When using local LLMs, your code, prompts, and context never leave your machine. Everything is processed locally for complete privacy.

Can I use online LLMs too?

Absolutely! Unlicode supports both local and online LLMs. You can choose based on your needs - local for privacy, online for convenience.

How do I get started?

Download Unlicode, install your preferred local LLM, and start coding! Check our documentation for detailed setup guides.

Is it really free?

Yes! The core Unlicode IDE is completely free and open source. Pro features are available for advanced users who want additional capabilities.
